About Me

Hi! My name is Thomas Ruohoniemi and I am a geography major at University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire. I plan to graduate in May 2024 with a degree in Geospatial Analysis and Technology.

Here you will find an assortment of works from various classes within the geography discipline, as well as a few I made on my own. I enjoy making all kinds of maps and I'm excited to showcase them here.
